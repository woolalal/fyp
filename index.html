ABOUT US

We are a group of final year students completing our studies at the University of Wollongong, working to develop SimpleBook, a website that can be used as an online appointment system for a health institution.

Our team strongly believes that health truly is wealth, and hope that through SimpleBook, users can achieve convenience by avoiding long waits at health institutions and live a healthier lifestyle through our health reminders.
